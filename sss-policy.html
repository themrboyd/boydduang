Privacy Policy for Stone, Sheet, Shears Game Extension

Last updated: 2024-13-07

Data Collection and Use
Our Stone, Sheet, Shears game extension collects and stores minimal data, all of which is kept locally on your device:

Player Name (optional): If you choose to enter a name, it's stored to personalize your game experience. Game Statistics: Your game scores and performance history are stored to track your progress.

Data Storage
All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's local storage API. We do not transmit or store any of your data on external servers.

Data Access and Control

  • You can view your stored data within the game interface.
  • You can reset all stored data at any time using the "Reset Score" function in the game.
  • Uninstalling the extension will remove all stored data.

Data Sharing We do not share any of your data with third parties. All information remains solely on your device.

Security
Since all data is stored locally, its security is managed by your browser's built-in security features.

Changes to This Policy
We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy in the extension's description on the Chrome Web Store.
