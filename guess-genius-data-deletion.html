GuessGenius - Data Deletion Instructions

How to Request Data Deletion

To request the deletion of your personal data from GuessGenius, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to tosaporn.boyd@gmail.com
  2. Use the subject line "Data Deletion Request for GuessGenius"
  3. In the body of the email, please provide your Facebook ID or the email address associated with your game account

What Data Will Be Deleted

Upon receiving your request, we will delete all personal data associated with your account, including:

  • Your game progress and scores
  • Any achievements or rewards earned
  • Your gameplay history
  • Any personal information used to personalize your game experience

Timeframe for Data Deletion

We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days of receiving your request.

Effects of Data Deletion

Please note that deleting your data will result in the loss of all game progress, achievements, and any virtual items associated with your account. This action cannot be undone.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about the data deletion process, please contact us at tosaporn.boyd@gmail.com.
