GuessGenius Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2024-22-07

Welcome to GuessGenius, a number guessing game Chrome extension. We are committed to protecting your privacy and providing a safe, enjoyable gaming experience.

1. Information We Collect

GuessGenius does not collect any personal information. We only store your game progress and high scores locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. This data never leaves your browser and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

2. How We Use Information

The locally stored game data is used solely to provide game functionality, such as tracking your progress and displaying your high scores. We do not use this information for any other purpose.

3. Data Security

Your game data is securely stored within your browser using Chrome's built-in security features. We do not have access to this data and do not transmit it over the internet.

4. Third-Party Access

We do not share any information with third parties because we do not collect or have access to any user data.

5. Children's Privacy

GuessGenius is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to clear your local game data at any time by uninstalling the extension or clearing your browser data.
